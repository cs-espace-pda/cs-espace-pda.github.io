{{site.data.str.pda}}


35 years of experience delivering high quality systems for Earth Observation & Science data processing and dissemination towards to NEWSPACE customers.
{{site.data.str.cs}}, a key European player in the field of satellite imagery and data processing, is positioned along the entire value chain, from sensors to applications. Through its {{site.data.str.pda}}, {{site.data.str.cs}} brings its expertise in remote sensing image processing, software development and, in particular, open source software. {{site.data.str.pda}} carries out the definition and development of entire systems, from image quality centers to value-added data production and exploitation centers.


{{site.data.str.pda}} responds to the increasingly massive needs for data exploitation (satellites & others) by providing appropriate IT environments and digital tools and being able to offer a variety of Big Data frameworks (and/or on-demand applications) adapted to needs without having to manage the allocation of the necessary IT resources.
{{site.data.str.pda}} is also involved in the development of web and GIS services for environmental applications.
Within the same department, {{site.data.str.pda}} brings together a multidisciplinary team of 120 engineers with different profiles - business, software, system - thus ensuring essential complementarity and enabling it to best meet the expectations of its customers, which are the CNES, the European Agency and the space industry.
